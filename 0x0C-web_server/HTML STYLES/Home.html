You are preparing to travel to Thailand for the first time.
1. How many months am  I planning to stay in Thailand? Convergent
2. How much will it cost? Evaluative
3. Do I have a place to stay or a friend to host me? Evaluative
4. Is Thailand the best place to visit? Provocative
5. What is the climate/weather in Thailand? Convergent
6. If I have a free visa to any country, will Thailand be the choice? Provocative
7. What if there are other places that offer better services, would Thailand still take the slot? Provocative
8. What will happen if my trip to Thailand is cancelled? Divergent
9. Why am I travelling to Thailand? Evaluative
10. Do I have all my travel documents complete? Evaluative
